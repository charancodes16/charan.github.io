Future Research Plans
I'm an incoming Ph.D. student in Information Sciences at the School of Information Sciences at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, where I'm fortunate to be advised by Dr. Michael Twidale.

My research lies at the intersection of AI and HCI, guided by the scientific vision of "Augmenting human intellect using machine intelligence."

My research will focus on Human-Computer Interaction, including:
Learning to Use: learning theories and training users
Novel interfaces with minimal information
Collaborative environments and social computing systems involving agents, simulations, and humans
Making intelligence in the sciences of artificial (a bit AI/ML)
