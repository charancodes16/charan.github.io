[1] Parental Collaboration and Closeness: A Co-Design Exploration with New Couple Parents

Ya-Fang Lin, Xiaotian Li, Wan-Hsuan Huang, Charan Pushpanathan Prabavathi, Jie Cai, John M. Carroll

ACM Designing Interactive Systems Conference DIS, 2025 [Under Review]

Existing technologies support parenting and collaboration but inadequately address closeness between co-parents, prompting an investigation through 10 co-design workshops with new parent couples. The study examined parents' current technology use, their adaptation of technologies for fostering closeness and interdependence, and identified opportunities for technology to better support emotional connection in co-parenting.

Explainable AI - XAI

[1] Trust and Decision-Making with Explainable AI in Immersive Technologies: A Systematic Literature Review

Hillmer Chona, Yihao Zhou, Ping Xu, Jeffrey Samuel Schulman Jr, Ting Yu Wu, Chenglin Weng, Siyu Wu, Charan Pushpanathan Prabavathi

Yet to Decide [Yet to Submit]

We conducted a systematic literature review analyzing 89 papers on trust and decision-making in XR environments, identifying key mechanisms for AI explainability and user trust calibration. Additionally, we analyzed the ethical implications of AI in XR applications, focusing on transparency, bias mitigation, and trust calibration strategies for effective human-AI collaboration.
