What drives my research:
  • I'm interested about research at the intersection of AI and HCI
  • Under a scientific vision that has captivated me: "Augmenting human intellect using machine intelligence"
  • This vision manifests in several focus areas I'm exploring:
    • Learning to Use: I'm fascinated by learning theories and how we can better train users to interact with complex systems
    • Novel interfaces with minimal information: How can we convey maximum meaning with minimal cognitive load?
    • Collaborative environments and social computing systems where agents, simulations, and humans work together seamlessly
    • Making intelligence in the sciences of artificial bridging the gap between AI research and practical applications
What I've come to believe about HCI:
  • Scaffolding is everything when it comes to learning
    • I've seen how well-designed support structures help users learn and accomplish tasks that seemed impossible at first
    • The beauty is in how these supports can be gradually removed as users become more proficient
    • This principle is absolutely critical for human-AI collaboration we need to scaffold the relationship
  • I believe interfaces should augment, never replace, human intelligence
    • Technology should amplify what humans do best, not try to substitute our unique capabilities
    • The goal I'm working toward is true symbiosis between humans and machines
    • Still finding the meaning of "intelligence" in this context
    • It's important to recognize that statistical mimicry is not the same as genuine intelligence or comprehension
  • Artificial psychology is a field that deserves more attention
    • I've learned that AI systems need psychological models to interact naturally with humans
    • Understanding how humans think about AI behavior is crucial for successful design
    • Users inevitably form theories about how systems work I believe we should intentionally design those theories
  • Computer supported cooperative work
    • I'm excited about multi-agent systems that seamlessly integrate humans and AI
    • Understanding cooperative activity and synergies has become central to my research
    • Social computing systems design fascinates me how do we create environments where different types of intelligence work together?
    • I envision environments where agents, simulations, and humans collaborate as equal partners
